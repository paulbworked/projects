Service Commands

Logical set of Pods and a policy for accessing them

Get service information about namespace or service:

kubectl get svc
kubectl describe svc
kubectl get svc -n datadog
kubectl get svc --all-namespaces
Kubectl get service | internal load balancer
kubectl get service ingress-nginx-controller -n ingress
kubectl describe service ingress-nginx-controller -n ingress

kubectl get services --namespace ingress-nginx -o wide -w ingress-nginx-controller
